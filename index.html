Who am I?

My name is Michael Troy Napolitano.
I was born and raised in Syracuse, New York.
I lived in a nice house with my wonderful family

My mother passed away in April 2016. I am the oldest
child in my family. I also have a small, brown dachshund.
Overall, my childhood was a pretty good one.

Education

I went to Cicero-North Syracuse High School for high school.
During my time there, I took some classes that would introduce
me to programming and all that it has to offer. One class showed me
how computers work and all the technicalities behind the machine.
I also took a class that showed me multiple programming languages
and the difference between them. Then, senior year I took AP Computer
Science. I always did exceptional in my classes and continuously opted
for the advanced placement of said classes. Currently, I'm studying at
University at Buffalo for a Bachelor's in Computer Science.

Why UB?

I chose University at Buffalo because I enjoyed every aspect of it
when I visited multiple times. I love that it's close to the city of
Buffalo and how large the campus is. The whole school feels like a
small city. I'm also very impressed by the professors and their classes.
Every one I met with was always eager to answer any question I had. The
classes here are also very satisfactory. They teach you the required material,
but incorporate it into interesting scenarios that always grab my attention.
I particularly enjoy my Computer Science class.
